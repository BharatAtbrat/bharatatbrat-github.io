About Intuitern
Intuitern is a small project by a student of Manipal University Jaipur, it aims to reduce the impact time when searching for a job or internships .

 

Why? Because searching and applying for jobs on different portals is hard. It takes a long time for someone to keep checking for new jobs, but this portal makes it easy and indexes and combines all of them for you at the click of a button.
